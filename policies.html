Store information

Shop policies

Please review our policies before placing your order. These policies are provided for demonstration purposes in this ecommerce project.

Shipping Policy

  • Orders are processed within 1-3 business days.
  • Estimated shipping time is 5-10 business days.
  • Shipping fees are calculated during checkout.
  • Delivery times may vary during holidays or peak seasons.

Return & Refund Policy

  • Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery.
  • Items must be unused and in original condition.
  • Customized products are non-refundable unless damaged.
  • Approved refunds are processed after item inspection.

Cancellation Policy

  • Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped.
  • Once an order is shipped, cancellation is no longer available.
  • Please contact support as soon as possible for cancellation requests.

Privacy Policy

  • We store customer information only for order processing.
  • Your name, email, and address are used for support and delivery.
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
  • This project uses localStorage for demo purposes.

Payment Policy

  • We support Cash on Delivery and mock online payment.
  • Orders are confirmed after checkout is completed.
  • Mock payment is used for demonstration in this project.
  • No real payment gateway is connected in this demo site.

Customer Support

  • Support is available during business hours.
  • You can reach us through the Contact page.
  • We aim to respond to inquiries within 24-48 hours.
